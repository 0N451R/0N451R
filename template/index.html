The Spider also has a retractable hardtop that can be opened or closed in just 14 seconds, allowing you to enjoy the thrill of driving with the wind in your hair. Inside, the cabin is luxurious and sporty, with premium materials and advanced technology. Overall, the Ferrari 488 Spider is a stunning example of Italian engineering and design.
